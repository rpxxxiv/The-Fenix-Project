JASON THOMAS: Lead Guitar and Vocals

Although Jason has a young face, he has been playing in Portland for nearly fifteen years, and is a premier "up and coming" local guitarist. He teaches guitar lessons by day, and plays in numerous rock, blues, and country bands weekly. JT has a special affinity for the blues, and plays with an obvious love. After being a regular at the jam, he has played with The Fenix Project for a couple years now.
