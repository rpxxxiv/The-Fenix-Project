JEFFERY OTTO: Keys and Vocals

Jeffery Otto has been working professionally in the Portland area for over fourty years. He has been with such acts as Buddy Knox, The Coasters, and The Drifters to name a few that have toured nationally and internationally.

Also worked in opening acts with Mellissa Etheridge, Tiffany, Richard Marks, The Backstreet Boys and toured with The Legends of Rock-N-Roll shows. He has also done a number of rock and roll impersonators such as Elvis, Roy Oberison, Buddy Holly and Patsy Cline.

He has worked with local belebrities Norman Sylvester, Lloyd Allen, Walter Guy, and Karen Maria Capo. In The Fenix Project he brings a ferocity to the keys in an unmatched way with mind blowing key solos. Jeffery is a graduate from the University of Portland class of 1980 with a Bachelors of Arts in Music.
