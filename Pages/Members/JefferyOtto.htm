FENIX: Saxphone and Lead Vocals

Hailing from Mississippi, Fenix has taken his gospel roots and smooth jazz saxaphone playing, combining them into something you simply must experience. Fenix has toured and has had the honor of playing with several notable national acts. Moved to Oregon in the early 90's and formed The Fenix Rising Band, a seven piece R&B and funk band, reinventing his sound.

The Fenix Project, his latest band, is a five piece featuring Fenix on vocals as well as soprano, alto, and tenor saxaphone, Jason Thomas on lead guitar and vocals, Fred Garner on bass, Jeff Otto on keyboard and vocals, and Jeff Frankel on drums.
